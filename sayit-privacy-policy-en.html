SayIt Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

SayIt app collects the following information:

  • Voice Recording Files: Stored on device and transmitted to server for STT conversion
  • Advertising ID (AAID): Used by Google AdMob for personalized advertising
  • Device Information: Android device ID, OS version, app version
  • App Usage Statistics: Anonymous usage patterns for app improvement

2. Purpose of Use

  • Providing speech-to-text conversion services
  • App functionality improvement and optimization
  • Providing personalized ads through Google AdMob
  • App performance analysis and bug fixes

3. Data Processing and Retention

Voice Data Processing:

  • All recording files are stored on the user's device
  • Files are encrypted and transmitted to our server (personak.duckdns.org) for STT conversion
  • Voice files are immediately deleted from server after text conversion (within 24 hours)
  • Users can delete recording files from their device at any time

Retention Period:

  • Voice files on device: Until user deletion
  • Voice files on server: Deleted immediately after conversion
  • Usage statistics and advertising data: 2 years

4. Third-Party Data Sharing

Personal information is shared with third parties only in the following cases:

  • Google AdMob: For advertising services
  • Google Play Services: For app analytics and performance improvement
  • When required by law

5. User Rights

Users have the following rights:

  • Right to access, correct, and delete personal information
  • Right to request suspension of personal information processing
  • Right to claim damages

6. Children Under 14

This app does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 14 years of age.

Response time: Within 3 business days
